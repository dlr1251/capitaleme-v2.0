What are the benefits and restrictions for this visa?

You can visit the country for up to 90 days

This visa can be valid for up to one (1) year. However, the authorized permanence in Colombian terriotorio will be 90 days (continous or discontinous). Those 90 days may be extended for 90 more. In any case, after staying in Colombia for more than 180 days in a 365 days period (starting from the date the visa was approved), you won‚Äôt be able to use this visa anymore and you will most likely have to either wait for the next year or to apply for a different visa category.

No beneficiaries

This visa does not allow applications for beneficiaries, that means that each family member needs to apply for this visa separately.

No work permit

This type of visa does not allow you to work in Colombia for Colombian companies, since it doesn‚Äôt have a work permit.

Since it is a visitor-type (V) visa, this won‚Äôt allow you to add time in order to apply for a resident (R) visa.

How to get the tourism visa

As with any visa application, the process is done online, through the Ministry for Foreign Relationship‚Äôs website. On this form, other than providing your personal, professional and family information and data, you will be required to attach some documents that evidence your tourism trip.

The specific requirements for a tourism visa may vary depending on your sponsorship. On your own behalf

Motivational letter

You will need to write a motivational letter informing your full identification (full legal name, passport number, birthdate, nationality and residency), explaining thoroughly and accurately the activities that you are planning to do under the tourism visa, including the address of where you will be staying (such as an hotel, hostal, etc), itinerary, participants, and any other relevant information that can give the authorities where can you be found, what will you be doing and with who.

Proof of your financial solvency

This can be accomplished by providing your bank statements from the previous six months, showing a monthly income of at least 10 Colombian minimum wages.

(As of 2023, the minimum wage in Colombia is $1.160.000 per month)

Sponsored by a Colombian host
